Instructions

  1. Proof the yeast

    2. Put the yeast into warm water

  2. Make the Pizza dough

    3. Assemble the Ingredients in a bowl and mix throughouly

  3. Knead the pizza dough

    4. Put dough onto a clean surface and fold it until its tacky to the touch

  4. Let the dough rise

    5. Place the dough into a warm and humid enviroment for at least 1 hour

  5. Assemble the Pizza

    6. Roll out the dough and put your desired Toppings on

  6. Bake the Pizza

    7. Set your oven to 250Â°C and bake for 10 to 15 minutes or until the crust is browned and the cheese melted
